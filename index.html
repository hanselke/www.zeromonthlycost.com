    Frequently asked questions and answers

    Why should I use your service instead of doing everything myself?

      Free website hosting services are only available for websites using static site technology and as of now, they all require web development skills to use.

  Our experienced web developers will be able to complete the project in a fraction of the time you would take

  We are constantly reviewing new free software and would add them into our bundle if they are useful. You are welcome to benefit from our research, the links to sign up yourself are all available on our main page.

    Why not use a wordpress template?

      We like wordpress templates, and do build wordpress sites for customers at the same cost.

  However, wordpress sites require a paid server to run, as well as regular updates and maintaince.
  We charge an additional USD$100/year for Wordpress Hosting & Maintenance

    Can you build me an E-commerce website?

      Shopping cart & User management functionalties are not available in static sites, and we recommend that you get a Shopify.com e-commerce website.

  We are Shopify partners, and would build you a Shopify site at the same cost.

  However, Shopify charges between $29 - $79 per month in subscription fees.

    What are the benefits of static website technology?

      They are very fast to load. 
  The output is plain html and does not require server side processing. Wix.com is based on static site technology.

  There is no web server to hack. 
  Almost all websites are hacked via a web server vulnerability.

  Free templates.
  Opensource static site frameworks have been around for about 10 years and there are thousands of free templates contributed by the very active community which includes Vox Media, Nest, Mailchimp and Carrot, a large New York design agency that is part of the Vice empire.

  Read more about it at https://www.smashingmagazine.com/2015/11/modern-static-website-generators-next-big-thing/
